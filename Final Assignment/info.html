A little bit about... History!

Canada was first discovered by europeans as they found that all the land was populated by natives. They also called those natives indians because the very first people to reach Canada thought they were at the East Indies. All the native people survived 100% from everything that was provided in those unknown lands. The arrival of Europeans affect the way of living of the natives in the area. The land was most colonized by british and French people which affected the official language of Canada nowadays. As years pass by and the process of colonization starts, british and french went on a head on conflict with each other and the British ended up victorious being responsible for most of the colonization which today resulted in Canada.

A little bit about... Travel tips!

If you love winter, then I recommend coming in the winter season if you don't I would recommend coming in the summer. Why that? because most attractions are completely closed in winter due to the unexpected weather which makes any schedule unpredictable. My travel tip is top always plan ahead since Canada has so many provinces and so many places to be, the first tip is to rent a car because most busses take forever here although you can get a presto card and do most trips for a reasonable price you will end up wasting a lot of time. If you're going to rent an apartment, rent one with a kitchen so you can cook, buying take out or going out to eat cost a lot of money around here. Always bring a change of clothes with you! sometimes even in summer it can get super cold because of the rain.

Why I chose this destination:

Since I was a child, I had a huge interest in Canada. One of the motives being the fact that I've never seen snow in my entire life since in my country It's never that cold. So for me Canada was always a wonderland, I went there my first time as an exchange student in 2015. Firstly, I went to vancouver as a teenager with average english skills. Canada gave me the freedom that I never had in my country. I could walk alone at night, walking alone wasn't scary as a woman and everything I ever imagined, although I may find those things in other places as well, my mind was set on Canada. Citizens are very welcoming and there is a huge variety of cultures living together, making the experience more spectacular
